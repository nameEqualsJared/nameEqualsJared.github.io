10 Simple Ways to Save Energy at College

  1. Turn off the lights when you leave the room.
  2. Take the stairs instead of the elevator.
  3. Use cold water for your laundry -- this saves energy because it does not require heating any water.
  4. Wash only full loads of clothes.
  5. Lower the brightness on your phone and laptop -- this not only saves energy, but it also extends your battery life!
  6. Instead of using the air conditioning unit, open a window.
  7. If your AC unit is on, keep your windows shut.
  8. Print on both sides of the paper.
  9. Turn off unused electronics -- standy power can account for 10% of an average household's power usage.
  10. Recycle -- reusing products means we won't need to use energy to recreate them.
